Privacy Policy

Lendly - Your Neighborhood Rental App

Effective Date: December 15, 2024

1Introduction

Lendly ("we", "our", or "us") is a peer-to-peer app that allows neighbors to rent items to each other. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use Lendly.

2Information We Collect

We use Firebase and AdMob, which may collect the following data:

  • Account Info: Email address and phone number used to log in.
  • Identifiers: Device ID and user ID (if assigned).
  • Usage Data: App launches, taps, clicks, scrolling, screen views, and interaction with ads.
  • Advertising Data: Ads seen or clicked, ad performance metrics.
  • Location Data: Your approximate location detected once you open the app to show nearby items.
  • Diagnostics: Crash logs, performance data, and technical diagnostics.

3How We Use Your Data

We and our third-party partners use collected data for:

  • Providing app functionality, including showing nearby rental items.
  • Displaying relevant ads via AdMob.
  • Analyzing app usage to improve features and user experience.
  • Monitoring app stability and performance.

4Data Sharing

We may share your data with:

  • Firebase for database and analytics services.
  • AdMob for ad targeting and analytics.
  • Other service providers who help us operate or improve the app.

5Location Data

We collect location data only when you open the app. This helps show rental items near you. Location data may be linked to your device ID for analytics and ad targeting.

6User Control

You can control certain data collection through your device settings, including limiting ad tracking, controlling location access, or resetting your advertising identifier.

7Children's Privacy

Lendly is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with the revised effective date.
